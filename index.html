About InAmigos

InAmigos (Friends for Positive Change) is a grassroots non-profit organization working since 2015 to improve the quality of life for marginalized communities. We operate with a simple philosophy: every individual deserves access to quality education, healthcare, and economic opportunities.

Our core values:

  • ✅ Transparency: Every action is accountable to our community
  • ✅ Compassion: We lead with empathy in all initiatives
  • ✅ Sustainability: Building long-term impact, not quick fixes
  • ✅ Community First: Communities lead their own development
  • ✅ Innovation: Using modern solutions for traditional problems

Since 2015, we have directly impacted over 50,000 lives across 150+ communities in Uttar Pradesh, Bihar, and Madhya Pradesh.

🎯 Our Mission

To empower disadvantaged communities by providing equitable access to education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities through innovative and sustainable programs.

✨ Our Vision

A world where every individual, regardless of socio-economic background, has the opportunity to realize their full potential and contribute meaningfully to society.

Why Our Projects Matter

🎓 Education Gap

50% of rural children drop out before high school. Our programs keep them in school and prepare them for the digital economy.

💚 Health Crisis

Rural areas lack basic healthcare. Our clinics and health camps save lives and prevent preventable diseases.

💼 Employment Challenge

Youth unemployment is at 23%. Our vocational training creates skilled workers and reduces poverty through employment.
